VTU CGPA Calculator - 2018 Scheme

Enter Semester wise SGPA

To calculate VTU CGPA, enter SGPA of each semster and hit on Calculate. CGPA Will be calculated and displayed. You can also calculate your CGPA as per your choice by entering 2 or 3 SGPA's.


The formula used to calculate VTU CGPA is :


∑(Ci x Si)

∑ Ci


Where Si = SGPA of the ith semster

Ci = Total Credits in the ith Semester
